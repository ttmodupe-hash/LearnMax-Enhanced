## Slide 2: The Challenge

**South African education faces significant challenges:**

- **Inequality**: A vast gap between rural and urban schools
- **Crime**: Device theft is a major barrier to digital learning
- **Resources**: Lack of technology, content, and support
- **Digital Divide**: Limited internet and high data costs
- **Teacher Burnout**: Overworked educators, lack of tools

---

## Slide 3: The Solution

**LearnMax Enhanced: A Holistic Educational Ecosystem**

| Challenge | Solution |
|---|---|
| **Inequality** | Offline-first, equal resources for all |
| **Crime** | 4-layer anti-resale, GPS tracking, remote disable |
| **Resources** | 8.5 GB pre-cached content, virtual labs, KPI dashboards |
| **Digital Divide** | Offline-first, low-data, SMS access, P2P sharing |
| **Teacher Burnout** | Automated KPI tracking, personalized study advice |
