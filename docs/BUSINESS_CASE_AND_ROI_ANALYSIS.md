## 1. Executive Summary

This document presents a compelling business case for the national rollout of the **LearnMax Enhanced** platform, demonstrating a significant **Return on Investment (ROI)** for the Department of Basic Education (DBE) and the South African economy. The analysis is based on a 3-year projection for a national rollout to **1 million learners**.

The total investment for this rollout is **R2.2 billion** over 3 years, with a projected **financial return of R4.1 billion** and a **Social Return on Investment (SROI) of R10.5 billion**, resulting in a combined **ROI of 6.6x**.

This investment will not only transform education but also create jobs, reduce crime, and contribute significantly to South Africa's economic growth.

## 4. Social Return on Investment (SROI)

### Total Social Value (3 Years): R10,500,000,000

### Social Benefits

| Benefit | Annual Value | 3-Year Value | Rationale |
|---|---|---|---|
