**To**: School Principals, SGBs, IT Administrators, and Educators

## 1. Introduction

This playbook provides a step-by-step guide for the successful deployment of the **LearnMax Enhanced** platform in your school. It is designed to be a practical, hands-on resource for all stakeholders involved in the rollout process.

Our goal is to ensure a smooth, efficient, and successful deployment that minimizes disruption and maximizes impact from day one.

---

## 2. Deployment Overview

The deployment process is divided into three main phases:

1.  **Phase 1: Pre-Launch (4 Weeks)** - Preparation and planning
2.  **Phase 2: Launch (1 Week)** - Device distribution and platform go-live
3.  **Phase 3: Post-Launch (Ongoing)** - Support, monitoring, and optimization

---

| **Professional Development** | LearnMax Team | Third week of month | ✅ Ongoing training sessions for educators |

### Device Loss/Theft Process

1.  **Learner Reports Loss/Theft**: Learner or parent reports loss/theft to school office.
2.  **School Logs Incident**: School logs incident in LearnMax portal.
3.  **Automatic Response**: System automatically:
    - Activates GPS tracking
    - Sends remote disable command
    - Blacklists IMEI
4.  **Insurance Claim Filed**: School files claim with self-insurance pool.
5.  **Replacement Device Issued**: Learner receives replacement device within 24-48 hours.
6.  **Learner Logs In**: Learner logs in, all data is restored from cloud.

## 7. Support Process

| Level | Support Provider | Responsibilities | Escalation Time |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Level 1** | IT Administrator | Basic troubleshooting, password resets, device issues | Escalate after 1 hour |
| **Level 2** | LearnMax Help Desk | Platform issues, software bugs, advanced technical support | Escalate after 4 hours |
| **Level 3** | LearnMax Engineering | Critical bugs, system outages, security incidents | Immediate escalation |
